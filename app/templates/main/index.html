About Me

I'm an AI and machine learning researcher passionate about using technology to make healthcare smarter and more accessible. My work focuses on medical data — from wearable sensors to biomedical images — building intelligent systems that help doctors make better, faster decisions. I love working at the intersection of medicine and AI, where complex problems meet creative solutions. Over the years, I've explored everything from detecting neurological disorders to generating synthetic medical data using GANs. What excites me most is seeing my research move beyond theory and into tools that can improve lives. Outside of work, you'll often find me exploring new tech trends, brainstorming ideas, or sharing knowledge with others who are just as curious about AI's potential in healthcare.
